Protein Cookies

Description

High protein waffles made using oats, cottage cheese, eggs, honey, vanilla extract, and cinnamon. Very healthy and taste delicious

Ingredients

  • 1/3 Cup Rolled Oats
  • 1/2 Medium Banana, Mashed
  • 1/2 Ounce Sugar Free Hot Cocoa Mix
  • 1/2 Scoop Chocolate Protein Powder
  • 1 Tablespoon Peanut Butter
  • 1 Tablespoon Apple Sauce
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Baking Powder

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
  2. Mix oats, banana, cocoa mix, protein powder, peanut butter, applesauce, and baking powder together in a bowl.
  3. Drop dough by spoonfuls onto a cookie sheet, 2 inches apart.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden, and set around the edges, 10 to 15 minutes.
